Daya Arts is a brand that sprung to life as a kid was bored at home after A/Ls. With a friend introducing her to Illustrator she ventured out to the world of digital art. Little did she know, it grew into the brand, "Daya Arts".

Daya Arts consists of a wide range of art forms ranging from vector arts to canvas paintings.
